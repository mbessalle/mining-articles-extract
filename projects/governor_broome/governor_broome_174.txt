3 August 2023
COMPLETION OF STRATEGIC FOURACRES HEAVY
MINERAL SANDS PROJECT ACQUISITION, WA
High-value mineral assemblage enhances Governor Broome Project
Further to its announcement dated 13 July 2023, Astute Metals NL (ASX: ASE, “Astute” or “the Company”)
is pleased to advise that it has completed the 100% acquisition of the Fouracres tenement (Tenement
No. R70/22), located adjacent to its Governor Broome Minerals Sands Project in WA. All conditions
precedent to the transaction have been fulfilled and ownership has now passed to Astute’s wholly owned
subsidiary, Governor Broome Sands Pty Ltd.
Background
The Governor Broome Heavy Mineral Sands Project is a largely de-risked resource development project
in the south-west of Western Australia with substantial total JORC Mineral Resource of 127Mt of Mineral
Sands grading 4.3% Heavy Minerals1. An updated table of project Mineral Resources is provided below.
Next Steps
In preparation for conducting a Q1 2024 Scoping Study for the Governor Broome Project, the Company is
actively advancing the following pre-requisite works and expected timing of market update is as follows:
• Mineral assemblage analysis for the recent Jack Track Deposit in-fill drilling program – Q3 2023
• Marketing study for mineral sands products – Q3 2023
• Bulk Sample Testwork Results for the Jack Track Deposit – Q3 2023
• Mineral Resource Estimate Update – Q4 2023
