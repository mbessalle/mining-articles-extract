The Silver Spoon project straddles a significant north-trending structural corridor, the Heathcote All Projects
Fault zone, that separates the Bendigo Zone to the west from the Melbourne Zone to the east
(Figure 1). Deformed and faulted Ordovician turbidites of the Bendigo Zone host high-grade quartz-
reef hosted gold mineralization at the Fosterville gold mine, located 20 km west of Silver Spoon.
