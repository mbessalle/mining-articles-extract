About Heavy Rare Earths Limited  
Heavy Rare Earths Limited (ASX: HRE) is an Australian uranium and rare earth exploration 
and development company. HRE’s uranium exploration projects are in the uranium-rich 
Curnamona Province of eastern South Australia and in the Mid West region of Western 
Australia. The Company’s key rare earth exploration project is Cowalinya, near Esperance in 
Western Australia.
