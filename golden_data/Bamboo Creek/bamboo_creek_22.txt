ABOUT NOVO April Corporate Presentation
Novo explores and develops its prospective land package covering approximately 10,500 square
kilometres in the Pilbara region of Western Australia, including the Beatons Creek gold project, along
with two joint ventures in the Bendigo region of Victoria, Australia. In addition to the Company’s
primary focus, Novo seeks to leverage its internal geological expertise to deliver value-accretive
opportunities to its stakeholders. For more information, please contact Leo Karabelas at (416) 543-
3120 or e-mail leo@novoresources.com.
